Unblocking Of VULMS Accounts
Published On: Tuesday, August 17, 2021

The University has unblocked LMS Accounts of all those students having outstanding dues up to Rs. 10,000/-, in order to give relaxation during COVID-19 situation and to facilitate them for making date sheet for Spring'2021 (Final-Term) Exams. For all those whose LMS Accounts are still blocked, It is advised to clear outstanding dues at the earliest or bring outstanding balance below Rs. 10,000/- to get LMS accounts unblocked.

It is further informed that LMS Accounts of all non-paying students will be blocked after final term exams.
